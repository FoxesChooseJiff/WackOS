/dev/fd(n) - Floppy disks where n is the number
/dev/void - Null pipe
/dev/com(n) - Serial communications port where n is is 1 to 4 (COM1 to COM4)
/dev/lpt(n) - Printer (parallel) ports where n is 1 or 2, or in rare cases 3 (LPT1 or LPT2, then LPT3)
/dev/mem - Physical memory "file". DANGEROUS!
/dev/textram - Text mode (B8000) video RAM
/dev/vgaram - VGA/Graphic mode (A0000) video RAM
